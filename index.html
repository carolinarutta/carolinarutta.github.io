My Skills

As a UX designer, I make products and technology usable, enjoyable, and accessible for for users. Most of the time, I work as part of a wider product team, and often find myself bridging the gap between the user, the development team, and key business stakeholders. As a UX designer, it’s my job first and foremost to advocate for the end user or customer. Whether I am designing a brand new product, coming up with a new feature, or making changes to an existing product or service—I consider what’s best for the user and the overall user experience. At the same time, I am also responsible for making sure that the product or service meets the needs of the business.
